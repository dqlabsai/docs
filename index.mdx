PRIZM is DQLabs' unified data quality and observability platform. It connects to your data ecosystem—Snowflake, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redshift, and more—so you can discover assets, monitor data quality in real time, get alerted when something goes wrong, and track issues to resolution. Whether you're a data engineer, analyst, or data steward, PRIZM gives you the visibility you need to trust your data.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to PRIZM">
    Navigate to your PRIZM instance and sign in with your email and password. First-time users can create an account from the login screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your asset catalog">
    Go to **Assets** to browse all tables, views, pipelines, and reports connected to your data ecosystem. Use the search bar or type filters to find specific assets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor metrics and alerts">
    Open **Metrics** for a real-time view of data quality scores, pipeline health, and system uptime. Check **Alerts** to see any active notifications that need your attention.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolve issues">
    Navigate to **Issues** to see all open data quality problems. Assign priorities, track progress, and mark issues resolved when your data is back in good shape.
  </Step>
</Steps>
