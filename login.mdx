title: "Sign in, reset password, and create a PRIZM account"
description: "Learn how to sign in to PRIZM with your email and password, recover a forgotten password, and create a new account from the login screen."
---

The PRIZM login screen is where you authenticate before accessing the platform. It also lets you recover your password or create a new account if you don't have one yet. This page covers each of those workflows.

## Sign in to PRIZM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your PRIZM instance URL">
    Open your web browser and navigate to the URL your team uses for PRIZM. This is typically provided by your administrator. The login screen displays the PRIZM logo, a welcome message, and fields for your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and password">
    Type your registered email address into the **Email** field, then enter your **Password**. Click the eye icon at the right edge of the password field to show or hide your password as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign in">
    Click the **Sign in** button. PRIZM authenticates your credentials and redirects you to the dashboard—or to the page you were trying to reach before you were sent to the login screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  PRIZM remembers the page you were trying to access before login. After a successful sign-in, you are automatically redirected to that page instead of the default dashboard.
</Note>

## Forgot your password

If you can't remember your password, click the **Forgot your password?** link on the login screen, below the **Sign in** button. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

## Create a new account

If you don't have a PRIZM account, click **Create new account** on the login screen. This option appears below the **Forgot your password?** link and the divider. Follow the prompts to register with your email address.

<Info>
  Account creation may require approval from your organization's administrator, depending on how your PRIZM instance is configured. Contact your admin if you don't receive access after registering.
</Info>

## After signing in

Once you're inside PRIZM, you have access to the full header navigation—Assets, Metrics, Alerts, Issues, and the More menu.

<Tip>
  Use the dark mode toggle in the top-right corner of the header to switch between light and dark themes. Click the moon icon next to the profile button to activate it.
</Tip>
