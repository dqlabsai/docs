PRIZM opens on the dashboard, your central starting point for monitoring data quality across your organization. From here, every major section of the platform — Assets, Metrics, Alerts, Issues, and Analytics — is one click away via the top navigation bar. This guide walks you through each element of the header so you can orient yourself quickly and move efficiently through the product.

## The navigation bar

The top navigation bar runs across the entire header and is always visible, regardless of which page you're on.

On the left side, the PRIZM logo anchors the header. Clicking it returns you to the dashboard from anywhere in the application.

The primary navigation items sit immediately to the right of the logo:

### The "More" menu

After the four primary navigation items, a **More** button with a chevron icon expands a dropdown menu that surfaces additional pages, including **Analytics**. Click **More** to open the dropdown, then click any item to navigate directly to that page.

## Global search

The search field in the upper-right corner of the header lets you find any asset across your connected data sources without first navigating to the Assets page.

- The field displays a magnifying glass icon on the left and the keyboard shortcut **⌘ K** on the right as a reminder.
- When you focus the search field, a suggestions panel appears showing your **Recent Searches** (such as "data quality score", "pipeline status", or "users table") and **Popular Assets** (such as `users_table`, `orders_table`, and `customer_data`).
- Click any suggestion to populate the field and run that search immediately.

<Tip>
Press **⌘ K** on Mac or **Ctrl+K** on Windows to open global search from anywhere in PRIZM without reaching for your mouse.
</Tip>

## Profile and settings controls

Two icon buttons sit to the right of the search field:

- **Dark mode toggle** — Click the moon icon to switch between light and dark themes. PRIZM remembers your preference across sessions.
- **Profile** — Click the person icon to access account settings and profile options.
